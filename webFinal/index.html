About Me:

I am a person who is really interested in the world of gaming. Gaming has gotten more popular over the year and I want people to learn and know more about each other in this world so I decided to create a gaming lounge with a website for you to shop your gears.

This place is consists of many different gaming lounge for hundreds of people to connect with each other and either play for fun or competitively. There is even a bar for you to enjoy and rest after long periods of gaming. If you are those kind of people who enjoy arcade games then this is also the place for you! There is an arcade room full of the older games that you love when you were young and technology were not as good.

While planning a time on this website, you can shop for your gaming gears too ! From best quality gaming gears to your favorite gaming figures/toys, you can order stuff here to help you get a higher quality gameplay or decorate your room with your favorite gaming figures
